LEARN DAVAO

Learn Davao is a web application that helps spread reliable information to Davaoenos to avoid the belief of fake news about the pandemic and the vaccine. It also updates the people of the current status of the city regarding the pandemic, this web application aims to help prevent the rise of COVID 19 cases by dispersing information and awareness.

NEEDS

This web application target’s the Davaoeno’s need of primary source of COVID related information in the city such as; high risk areas, vaccination site, number of cases and Tips and Guidelines.

SOLUTION

- Learning Technology provides reliable source of information regarding COVID - 19.
- It will guide Davaoeno’s to avoid high risk areas.
- To know the number of cases within the area.

DIFFERENTIATION

- Our Web application focus only in local area.
- Our web application cannot be covered up by any information that is unrelated to COVID - 19 since only focuses in the said topic.

BENIFITS

- Update the Davaoeno’s risk status through online web application.
- It is free access to all.
- Spread awareness to the local people which can help reduce the COVID case.
